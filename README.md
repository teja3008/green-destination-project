# 🌿 Green Destinations Employee Attrition Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition at **Green Destinations**, a travel agency experiencing an increase in employee turnover. The objective is to identify trends and factors influencing attrition using interactive Tableau visualizations.

The dashboard helps HR understand which employees are more likely to leave and what factors contribute to attrition.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Calculate the employee attrition rate.
- Analyze employee attrition across different demographics.
- Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard for HR decision-making.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- ✅ Overall Attrition Rate
- 👨‍💼 Gender-wise Attrition
- 🏢 Department-wise Attrition
- 🎂 Age-wise Attrition
- 💰 Monthly Income Analysis
- ⏰ Overtime Analysis
- 📈 Years at Company Analysis
- 📉 Years Since Last Promotion
- 📊 Attrition Distribution
- 📌 KPI Cards (Total Employees, Male, Female, Attrition Count)

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- Tableau Public
- Microsoft Excel
- CSV Dataset

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains employee information including:

- Age
- Gender
- Department
- Monthly Income
- Daily Rate
- Hourly Rate
- Education
- Job Role
- Overtime
- Years at Company
- Years Since Last Promotion
- Years with Current Manager
- Attrition Status

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Employees working overtime show a higher attrition rate.
- Most employees leaving the company are in the early years of employment.
- Monthly income has a noticeable relationship with employee retention.
- Some departments experience higher attrition than others.
- Younger employees are more likely to leave compared to senior employees.
